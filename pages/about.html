Our Story

Freshlane was founded in 2024 with a passion for stylish and affordable streetwear. Our goal is to create quality hoodies and cargo pants that let every youth express their unique style while walking their lane.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower youth with trendy, comfortable clothing that boosts confidence and individuality.

Vision: To be South Africa's go-to streetwear brand that combines style, comfort, and affordability.
